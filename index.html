Campaign Planning & Execution Framework

Phase 1: Discovery & Setup (Weeks 1-2)

  • Influencer Database Build: Identify 100+ potential influencers across all geos
  • Audience Analysis: Verify follower demographics, engagement rates, authenticity
  • Compliance Audit: Ensure influencers meet regulatory requirements per geo
  • Contract Templates: Prepare geo-specific agreements with disclosure requirements
  • Content Guidelines: Create brand guidelines and compliance checklists

Phase 2: Outreach & Activation (Weeks 3-4)

  • Personalized Outreach: Contact top 50 influencers with tailored proposals
  • Negotiation: Finalize rates, deliverables, and timelines
  • Onboarding: Brief influencers on DGbet brand, compliance, content expectations
  • Content Calendar: Schedule posts across geos for maximum impact
  • Tracking Setup: Implement UTM codes, promo codes, affiliate links

Phase 3: Campaign Launch (Weeks 5-8)

  • Staggered Rollout: Launch by geo to manage resources and optimize
  • Content Approval: Review all influencer content pre-publication for compliance
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Track engagement, clicks, conversions hourly
  • Community Management: Respond to comments, questions, engagement
  • Performance Reporting: Weekly dashboards with key metrics

Phase 4: Optimization & Scale (Weeks 9-12)

  • Data Analysis: Identify top-performing influencers and content types
  • Budget Reallocation: Double down on high-ROI partnerships
  • A/B Testing: Test different messaging, CTAs, content formats
  • Expansion: Add 10-15 new influencers based on learnings
  • Ambassador Program: Convert top performers to long-term ambassadors

Compliance Safeguards

  • Pre-Approval Process: All content reviewed before posting
  • Disclosure Requirements: Mandatory #ad, #sponsored tags per geo
  • Age-Gating: No influencers with >100K under-18 followers (UK/EU)
  • Geo-Blocking: Content restricted in non-licensed jurisdictions
  • Responsible Gaming: All content includes responsible gambling messaging
